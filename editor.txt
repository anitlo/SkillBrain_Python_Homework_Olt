IT SERVICE AGREEMENT

PARTIES
1.1. Provider: Tech Solutions Ltd.
1.2. Client: Acme Corp.

DATA PROCESSING
2.1. Provider shall process “personal data” only with explicit “consent” of data subjects.
2.2. Data will not be retained “in perpetuity” and will be deleted upon request (“right to erasure”).
2.3. In case of a “data breach,” Provider will notify Client within 72 hours.

PURPOSE LIMITATION
3.1. Personal data may be used only for the purposes agreed in writing, never “for any purpose” outside that scope.

SECURITY
4.1. Provider implements “appropriate technical and organizational measures” to protect data.
